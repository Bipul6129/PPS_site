Who we are

We offer a turnkey service, the execution of the project from the initial design to the final construction & handover of the space. As a Professional & Reliable Contracting Company we are proud to offer our services all across Nepal. Our experienced workforce provides unparalleled service, and focuses on delivering superior quality combined with long-lasting results. Our team have the capability to undertake a wide range of projects, varying in size and specifications, and we offer competitive prices, alongside flexible payment plans, to secure projects for a range of budgets. Whether your premise is an office, retail store, restaurant, hotel, or residence, we are qualified to offer you expert advice ensure that your space embodies your desires, ethos and brand identity. We provide all of our clients with a dedicated project team who work to complete the interior fit out works within the specific time, standards, and budget.
